About

I have been working as a full-time tennis umpire since 2013 and have traveled to more than 40 countries around the world. During the pandemic I stayed at home in Gothenburg and fell in love with my home town, I realized how much I enjoy being at home so I started thinking that maybe there are more things than tennis in my life. It was time to try something new.

In january 2021 I started working for Gelateria Positano as a store manager and chief of staff. It was nice to try something else, I enjoyed meeting costumers and taking care of all employees. However, being responsible for three stores and 20+ employees + working full time in the store was heavy and I could never stop thinking about my job. After a few months I injured my wrist scooping gelato. That's when I knew that this was not the right place for me to be. I began thinking about education and what I wanted to do when I came across Medieinstitutet.

During my trip to Japan working the 2020 Tokyo Paralympic Games I started to prepare for the next chapter in my life, the road of becoming a Front End Developer.
